The Collection Menu is an additional tab on the main menu that records and allows the player to view the contents of Colored Pearls that have been transcribed within Story or Expedition Mode, in addition to the collectible Broadcasts within Spearmaster's campaign. The interface consists of a vertical 5 by 13 grid of buttons on the left side, for a total of 65 entries (30 unique Pearls and 35 Broadcasts). Each entry is initially undiscovered and blanked out with a question mark, and once discovered, becomes interactable buttons labeled with a colored icon of the respective Pearl / Broadcast. On the right is a readout of the currently selected entry, which displays "[ Collection Empty ]" when none are selected.
